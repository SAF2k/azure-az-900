# 🛡️ Azure Governance and Compliance

Effective governance ensures your Azure environment stays secure, compliant, and aligned with business goals.

---

## 📋 Key Governance Tools

- **Azure Policy:**  
  Enforce rules and effects on resources to ensure compliance with organizational standards.

- **Management Groups:**  
  Organize subscriptions into hierarchical containers for unified policy and access management.

- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):**  
  Assign precise permissions to users and groups to enforce the principle of least privilege.

---

## 📝 Compliance Certifications

Azure complies with global standards such as:

- GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)  
- ISO 27001  
- HIPAA  
- FedRAMP  

This helps organizations meet regulatory requirements easily.

---

## 🔒 Security Center and Blueprints

- **Azure Security Center:**  
  Provides unified security management and threat protection.

- **Azure Blueprints:**  
  Automate deployment of compliant environments with pre-built templates.
